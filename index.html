Artist Identity

Yashvardhan is an independent Desi Hip Hop artist From Diu representing raw emotion, real struggle, and self-made hustle. Without industry backing or nepotism, his journey is built from passion, discipline, and consistency.

Inspired by India's evolving hip hop movement, his music reflects middle-class reality, ambition, loneliness, and the mindset of artists building legacy from zero support.
